About Me

Born and raised in Tbilisi, Georgia, I am a passionate advocate for environmental sustainability and international diplomacy. As a high school senior at LEPL Tbilisi Public School N167, I balance rigorous academics with leadership roles in various initiatives that bridge environmental activism with diplomatic engagement.

My journey began with local environmental projects, but quickly evolved into a broader mission of fostering global understanding and cooperation. I believe that environmental challenges require international solutions, which has led me to pursue both environmental activism and diplomatic endeavors.
